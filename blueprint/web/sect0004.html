3 Proof of the Main Theorem

3.1 High-Level Strategy

The proof proceeds in the following steps:

  1. Prove Selberg’s identity (??lem:selberg).

  2. Use the identity to show that \(\psi (x) = x + o(x)\).

  3. Conclude the PNT via the equivalence of Chebyshev functions (??lem:pnt_equivalence).

3.2 From Selberg to PNT

Proof of ??thm:daboussi_pnt ▶

Assume we have established Selberg’s identity (??lem:selberg). We then perform a delicate analysis of the error terms to show that

\[ \psi (x) = x + o(x). \]

By ??lem:pnt_equivalence, this implies

\[ \pi (x) \sim \frac{x}{\log x}, \]

which is the desired result.
