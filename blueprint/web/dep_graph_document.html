Lemma 3Equivalence of PNT forms

The following are equivalent:

  1. \(\pi (x) \sim \frac{x}{\log x}\),

  2. \(\psi (x) \sim x\),

  3. \(\vartheta (x) \sim x\).

Lemma 2Selberg’s Identity

For \(x \geq 1\), we have

\[ \psi (x) \log x + \sum _{n \leq x} \Lambda (n) \psi \left(\frac{x}{n}\right) = 2x \log x + O(x), \]

where \(\psi \) is Chebyshev’s psi-function and \(\Lambda \) is the von Mangoldt function.

Theorem 1Daboussi’s Prime Number Theorem

Let \(\pi (x)\) denote the number of primes less than or equal to \(x\). Then

\[ \lim _{x \to \infty } \frac{\pi (x) \log x}{x} = 1. \]
